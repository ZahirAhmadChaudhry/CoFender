Is the AI's analysis legally binding?

The AI provides preliminary analysis only. Every document receives human lawyer verification before any certificate is issued. The lawyer's signature creates the legally relevant audit trail, not the AI.

What qualifications do your reviewing lawyers have?

All reviewing lawyers are licensed in France with specialization in data protection law. They maintain professional liability insurance and follow established review protocols.

Analysis Complete

2 potential issues found

1. Missing DPO Contact Information

The document mentions data processing but fails to specify a Data Protection Officer contact as required by GDPR Article 13.

2. Vague Data Retention Period

Clause 4.2 states "data will be kept for as long as needed" which is too vague under GDPR storage limitation principles (Art. 5).

How to navigate and test the pilot features.

Accountant View (You)

This is your default dashboard where you manage client documents.

  • Upload: Click "New Document" to start a compliance check. You can upload any PDF/DOCX (simulated).

Simulate the lawyer's experience to see how they validate your files.

  • Queue: See documents sent by accountants.
